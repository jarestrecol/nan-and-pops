Why Choose Nan & Pops?

It is choosing quality, connection, and an environment designed to make everyone feel welcome.

A Place to Share Together

We’ve created a warm and welcoming space where families can enjoy time together, laugh, talk, and make lasting memories. Every visit becomes a special moment for both kids and adults.

Pet Friendly

We welcome your furry friend with open arms, offering cozy spaces, pup cups and fresh water bowls so everyone feels at home.

Pay Your Way

Cash, card, or contactless—we’ve got you covered.

Fun for Little Artists

We offer creative activities for kids, including coloring pages and crayons. A cheerful space where they can play, draw, and enjoy their visit while you relax.

Barrier-Free Access

Our shop offers safe and comfortable access for individuals with reduced mobility. Everyone is welcome to enjoy their visit without obstacles.

Park with Ease

We offer convenient parking spaces right in front of our shop so your visit is stress-free. Just pull up and enjoy—your spot is waiting!

Nan & Pop's is Entering a New Chapter

We are excited to introduce ourselves as the new owners of Nan & Pop’s. We are dedicated to preserving the quality you know while bringing our own energy to the shop. We believe in keeping things simple and delicious. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick treat or catching up with friends, we are here to ensure every visit is a good one. Come by and say hello.
